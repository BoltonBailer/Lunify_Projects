Intro: Hello World — I'm Marcos

Hey everyone,I’m Marcos, and this is the start of my little corner on the web. I’ve always loved mixing creativity with technology—whether it’s coding something weird at 2 a.m., building data science tools for Old School RuneScape, or just chasing ideas that keep me up way too late.

My journey here has been a mix of exploration and leveling up my skills. I’m currently studying Computer Science with a focus on AI & Data Science, and I’ve spent the past couple of years building projects that bridge gaming, design, and machine learning. Honestly, half the fun for me is turning serious concepts into playful, creative experiments.

This blog is where I’ll share that journey with you—my wins, my fails, and everything else. My goal is to document my growth, get more consistent with building things in public, and maybe inspire someone else to take that first step too.

Future posts are going to dive into real problems I’m working on and the strategies I use to solve them, whether that’s debugging cursed code, training a model, or finding clever ways to make apps smarter. But for now… this is the beginning. Thanks for being here
